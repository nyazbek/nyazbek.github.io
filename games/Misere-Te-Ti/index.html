☠ How to Play

The Twist

This is Misère Tic-Tac-Toe — the rules are reversed. The player who completes 3 in a row LOSES. You must force your opponent to make a line!

Turns

Players alternate placing their mark. The board is 3×3. You cannot pass.

Winning

  • If your opponent completes 3 in a row → you win.
  • If you complete 3 in a row → you lose.
  • If the board fills with no 3-in-a-row → draw.

Strategy

Force your opponent into corners. Avoid creating two-in-a-row setups that give you no escape. The center is dangerous — it connects 4 winning lines!
