⬛ How to Play

The Setup

The board is a 3×3×3 cube — three stacked layers, each a 3×3 grid. Cells are addressed by (layer, row, column).

Goal

Get 3 of your marks in a row in any direction through the 3D space. Lines can be horizontal (within a layer), vertical (through layers), or diagonal (cutting through all three layers).

Winning Lines (49 total)

  • 8 per layer × 3 layers = 24 flat lines
  • 9 vertical lines (same row/col through all layers)
  • 16 diagonal lines cutting across layers

Tips

The center cell of the middle layer participates in 13 winning lines — control it early. Space diagonals (corner-to-corner through the cube) are the hardest to block.
