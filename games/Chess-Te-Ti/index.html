Four in a row — brilliant!

♟ How to Play

Objective

Line up 4 of your pieces in a row — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally on the 4×4 board.

Phases

  • Rounds 1–3: Place any piece from your hand onto an empty square.
  • Round 4+: Place pieces or move pieces already on the board.

Your Pieces

Pawn — 1 step forward, captures diagonally. Bounces at edges!
Rook — any distance in a straight line
Knight — L-shape jump, leaps over pieces
Bishop — any distance diagonally

Captures

Capturing an enemy piece sends it back to their hand — they can place it again!
